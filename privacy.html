Privacy Policy for Allegra Connect

Effective date: 3 April 2026

Allegra Connect is an internal hotel operations integration used to connect ChatGPT-based assistants and custom GPTs to Resly for Allegra Hervey Bay. This Privacy Policy explains what data may be processed when the service is used.

1. Information processed

When Allegra Connect is used, it may process reservation and guest servicing information made available through the Resly account connected to the service. This can include guest names, booking identifiers, arrival and departure details, room information, communication records, and guest charge information.

2. Purpose of processing

The service processes data solely to support hotel operations, including reading reservations, creating reservations, reading and creating guest messages, and creating guest charges through authorized workflows.

3. Data sources

Data is obtained from Resly through the hotel's authorized API credentials and from user prompts submitted through ChatGPT or connected GPT interfaces.

4. Sharing of data

Data is not sold. Data is shared only with the systems required to operate the integration, which may include OpenAI-hosted interfaces, Vercel-hosted application infrastructure, and Resly as the property management platform.

5. Security

API credentials are stored as server-side environment variables and are not exposed to end users. Reasonable technical measures are used to protect the service and associated data flows.

6. Retention

Data retention depends on the underlying systems used by the hotel, including Resly, OpenAI, and Vercel. Allegra Connect is intended to act as a processing layer rather than a long-term system of record.

7. Access and correction

Requests relating to reservation or guest data should be directed to the hotel operator responsible for the connected Resly account.

8. Contact

For questions about this Privacy Policy or the Allegra Connect integration, contact the operator of Allegra Hervey Bay or the administrator of the connected system.

9. Changes

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time to reflect service changes, legal requirements, or operational improvements. The latest version will be published at this page.
